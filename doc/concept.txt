Heroes of Wealth and Justice

RPG-strategy hybrid, heroes must become rulers of their region to claim it for your faction. 

- multiple heroes in a single game (one for each region)
- each can have items, spells and followers
- fast characters progress
- when hero reach max level (12), he can become regional ruler and marry to breed a successor
- then hero's child takes over and is supposed to reach max level and claim another region
- meanwhile other factions are claiming regions as well
- ruling heroes can fight epic battle to take region from other faction

Generator
---------

- choose hero
  - Male/Female
  - Man/Dwarf/Elf/Halfling/Draconian/Orc
  - Fighter/Ranger/Mage/Cleric
  - Bandits/Priests/Kingdom/Tribe 

- race determines start location:
  - Beach/Shore (Men)
  - Woods (Halflings/Elves)
  - Mountains (Dwarves)
  - Underground (Draconian/Orc)

Faction
 - race
 - type (Bandits/Priests/Kingdom/Tribe)
 - leader

- randomly generate story hallmarks
 - Factions: enemy, friendly and neutral
 - Adversary: enemy faction leader or dragon/demon (most of the game you chase this)
 - Real Threat: powerful wizard or god or magical being (you defeat this in the endgame)
 - Mentor: leader of your or friendly faction, or some deus ex machina
 - Other world: another world, deep cave or secluded region
 - Elixir: weapon or some artifact, kidnapped king etc.

Departure

- meet your mentor and know your enemy

Initiation

- travel to other world
- find the elixir

Return

- return with elixir and defeat the adversary
- new enemies and other changes to the old world
- defeat the real threat


"Adversary"

One of other factions want your faction eradicated.

"Save the King"

Someone kidnapped your leader or stolen an artifact.

"Royal Heir"

You are lost heir to the throne.
